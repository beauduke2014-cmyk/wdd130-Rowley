Stories and poems

The Lantern Beneath the Lake
Genre: Gothic Fantasy

When a drought reveals a submerged village beneath a forgotten lake, a grieving historian discovers a lantern that reveals the past unseen. As she follows its glow, she uncovers a buried kingdom hidden between the living and the drowned.

Thread Logic
Genre: Science Fiction Mystery

In a future where thoughts are stitched into wearable fabrics, a rogue archivist unravels a thread that shouldn’t exist — one that drops him in the middle of a secret murderers’ machinations.

The Orchid that Ate the Moon
Genre: Surreal Fantasy

A boy plants a seed that grows into a glittering orchid. Each night, the moon shrinks in size, and in his dreams, the flower speaks of the wishes the moon never answered.

A Crowns Hollow Promise
Genre: Political Fantasy

In a realm where rulers are bound by magical contracts, a scribe discovers a coupe set in motion by another vindictive scribe. He must stop the coupe, or the entire order of the kingdoms could fall apart.

Games

Signal Drift

Skills: Puzzle Logic, Environmental Storytelling, Open-World Exploration Signal Drift is an exploration game set in a decaying digital city where players navigate signal towers, decode corrupted transmissions, and uncover fragments of a lost network. The game experiments with ambient storytelling, glitch aesthetics, and modular signal puzzles. Designed as a prototype for testing pacing, atmosphere, and non-verbal narrative delivery.
